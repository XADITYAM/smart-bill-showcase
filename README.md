# Smart Bill

Smart Bill is a Flutter-based billing and invoice management application designed for small businesses and shop owners. The application simplifies invoice creation, customer management, product tracking, and PDF invoice generation through a clean and user-friendly interface.

## Features

* Create and manage invoices
* Add, edit, and delete products
* Customer management system
* Invoice history tracking
* Generate professional PDF invoices
* Offline data storage using SQLite
* Simple and intuitive user interface
* Fast invoice generation workflow

## Key Functionalities

### Product Management

Store and manage products with pricing information for quick invoice generation.

### Customer Management

Maintain customer records and associate them with invoices.

### Invoice Generation

Generate invoices efficiently by selecting products and customers.

### PDF Export

Create professional PDF invoices that can be shared or printed.

### Invoice History

Access previously generated invoices for future reference.
